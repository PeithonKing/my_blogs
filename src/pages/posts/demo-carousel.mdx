# The Power of Grouping

When writing technical blogs, we often have a series of images that belong together. Perhaps it's a step-by-step tutorial, a comparison of results, or just a collection of moments from a project.

Traditionally, we'd stack them vertically, which kills the rhythm of the reading. Today, I'm introducing the **Carousel** componentâ€”a way to group these moments into a single, cohesive unit.

## Why This Matters

By grouping these 5 images, we saved about **2000 pixels of vertical vertical scrolling**. The narrative remains uninterrupted.

Clicking any image above opens the immersive view, allowing you to inspect the details without losing your place in the article. This is the balance between _evidence_ and _story_.
